Capstone day

Final assessment · Half-day session
Full AEO/GEO strategy
presentation & peer review

The capstone is a dedicated half-day, not a module session. Learners present a complete AEO/GEO audit and 6-month strategy for their own site. Peer panel review with a published rubric. Grading criteria shared with every learner before the day.

Part 1 — Full site audit (60 min)
Technical readiness, content quality, authority signals, current SoMR baseline
Part 2 — Strategy presentation (45 min)
6-month roadmap, prioritised fixes, resource requirements, KPI targets
Part 3 — Peer panel Q&A (30 min)
Structured peer review using provided rubric. Instructor facilitates
Part 4 — Executive summary (45 min)
One-page brief for CMO/board. Justifies AEO investment in business language
Grading rubric
Pass: solid audit + coherent roadmap. Merit: original insight + industry context. Distinction: measurable hypothesis + exec-quality presentation
Facilitator guide
Timing guide, common stumbling blocks, intervention cues, and debrief structure provided in separate appendix
